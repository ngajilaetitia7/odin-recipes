Baked Tomato Slices

Baked tomato slices are a delicious way to top leftover pasta!

Submitted by Laura, Updated on November 21, 2024


Preparation Time

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 5 mins
  • Total Time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided, or as needed
  • 1 large tomato, cut into 1/2-inch-thick slices
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves stripped and finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Brush a baking sheet with about 1 tablespoon olive oil.

Step 2: Arrange tomato slices on the baking sheet; sprinkle rosemary and garlic on top. Brush with remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil; season with salt and pepper.

Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
