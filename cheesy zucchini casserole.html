Cheesy Zucchini Casserole

This zucchini casserole with bread cubes, onion, and Cheddar is simple and easy to make for a deliciously cheesy vegetable side dish.

Submitted by DC1, Updated on November 21, 2024


Preparation Time

  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Cook Time: 1 hr
  • Total Time: 1hr 15 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bread, cubed
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 2 cups cubed zucchini
  • 1 large onion finely chopped
  • 1 large egg beaten
  • ½ teaspoon garlic salt or to taste
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

Step 1: Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2: Place bread cubes in a medium bowl and pour melted butter over top. Add zucchini, onion, egg, and garlic salt; mix well.

Step 3: Transfer the mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish and top with cheese.

Step 4: Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Uncover the dish and bake for another 30 minutes.
